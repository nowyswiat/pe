Piano Exercises
This application is designed to help you practise piano exercises (scales, arpeggios, etc.) for Trinity or ABRSM exams.
It will make suggestions about what to practise next, and then ask you to rate yourself for that particular exercise. Rate yourself on a scale of 0 to 5 (5 being best).
Another exercise will then be chosen for you randomly, but biased according to your progress, and to give variety.
The application can play the exercise to you, so you can get an idea of how your performance should sound. (Some exercises cannot be played as they are based on copyrighted material.)
Use Settings to select the organisation, syllabus, and grade you are studying.
